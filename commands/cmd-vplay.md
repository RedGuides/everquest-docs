# /vplay

## Syntax

<!--cmd-syntax-start-->
```eqcommand
/vplay <1-12>
```
<!--cmd-syntax-end-->

## Description

<!--cmd-desc-start-->
Play a voice macro for your computer only.
<!--cmd-desc-end-->

## Options

**`<1-12>`**
:   Play a voice macro, number 1 through 12.  
    ![The voice macro window](Voicemacros.jpg)

## Examples

!!! example "`/vplay 2` - Will sound 'Attack!' only on your computer."
